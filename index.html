My Dearest Love

You care so much about my health and wellbeing, and instead of listening to your loving advice, I went ahead and did exactly what you warned me not to do. I can only imagine how it must feel to see someone you love repeatedly making the same mistakes that could have been so easily avoided.

I promise you, from the bottom of my heart, that I will be more mindful and responsible about my health. You deserve a boyfriend who listens to your concerns and takes better care of himself – not just for my own sake, but because I know how much it worries you when I'm unwell.

Thank you for being patient with me, for caring so deeply, and for loving me even when I'm being irresponsible. I'm lucky to have someone who cares about me as much as you do. I love you more than words can express, and I'm sorry for causing you stress and worry.

Forever yours,
Your apologetic boyfriend 💙
